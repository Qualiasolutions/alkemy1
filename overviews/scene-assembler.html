Scene Assembler System

Shot-by-Shot Production Studio with AI Image & Video Generation

What is Scene Assembler?

The Scene Assembler is your shot-by-shot production workspace. Generate hero images for each frame, refine them iteratively, animate stills into motion video, and build complete scenes with professional camera control specifications.

Complete Shot Production Workflow

1
Select Scene & Frame
Choose scene from analyzed screenplay. View shot grid and select frame to enter dedicated workspace.
2
Configure Shot
Select characters (LoRAs auto-applied), location, aspect ratio, AI model, and number of variants (1-10).
3
Generate Hero Image
AI creates still image variants based on shot description, character identities, location context, and moodboard references.
4
Refine (Optional)
Enter Refine Studio for prompt-based improvements. Make lighting warmer, add fog, adjust composition. Unlimited iterations.
5
Animate to Video
Convert still image to 5-10 second video clip using Veo 3.1 or Kling AI. Provide motion prompt like "camera pushes in slowly".
6
Upscale & Export
Optional 4K enhancement for both images and videos. Transfer completed shots to Timeline for final editing.

Camera Package System

Each shot includes professional cinematography specifications that guide generation and inform crew:

Technical Specifications

  • Lens: Focal length in mm (24mm, 35mm, 50mm, 85mm, 200mm)
  • Aperture: f-stop value (f/1.4, f/2.8, f/5.6, f/11) for depth of field control
  • ISO: Sensor sensitivity (100, 400, 800, 1600) for exposure and grain
  • Height: Camera angle (Eye Level, Low, High, Bird's Eye)
  • Angle: Shot composition (Wide, Medium, Close-Up, Extreme Close-Up)
  • Movement: Camera motion (Static, Pan, Tilt, Dolly, Crane)
