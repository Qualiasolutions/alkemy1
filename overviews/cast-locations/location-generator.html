What is the Location Generator?

The Location Generator creates stunning, production-ready environments for any setting imaginable. From modern cityscapes to fantasy realms, generate detailed locations with perfect lighting, atmosphere, and architectural details that match your story's visual requirements.

Location Generation Workflow

1
Location Details
Enter location name, type (indoor/outdoor), time period, architectural style, and setting description.
2
Visual Specifications
Define lighting conditions, weather, time of day, atmosphere, mood, and specific architectural elements.
3
Context Integration
Connect to story requirements, character interactions, plot points, and practical production needs.
4
AI Model Selection
Choose appropriate model based on location type: photorealistic for contemporary, artistic for stylized settings.
5
Generate Variations
Create multiple angles, lighting conditions, and compositions to find the perfect shot for each scene.
6
Refine & Detail
Use Refine Studio to adjust specific elements: enhance lighting, add atmospheric effects, modify architectural details.
