What is the Character Generator?

The Character Generator creates custom characters for your production using advanced AI models. Generate unlimited character variations with consistent appearance, personality traits, and visual styling that perfectly match your creative vision.

Character Generation Workflow

1
Character Details
Enter character name, age, physical description, personality traits, and role in the story.
2
Visual Style
Choose appearance details: hair color, eye color, clothing style, facial features, and distinctive characteristics.
3
AI Model Selection
Select from Flux Pro 1.1, Flux Dev, Gemini Flash Image, or Imagen 4.0 based on quality vs speed needs.
4
Generate Variants
Create 1-10 character images with slight variations in expression, pose, and lighting for scene diversity.
5
Select Hero Image
Choose the best image as the character's hero appearance. This will be displayed in presentations and storyboards.
6
Identity Training
Optional: Train LoRA model for 90-98% visual consistency across all future generations.

AI Models & Capabilities

Flux Pro 1.1

Highest quality photorealistic characters with exceptional detail and lighting accuracy.

Flux Dev

Fast iterations for character development. Perfect for concept exploration and rapid prototyping.

Gemini Flash Image

Multimodal editing with reference images. Refine existing characters or match specific visual styles.

Imagen 4.0

Google's text-to-image model for pure prompt-based character generation with creative interpretation.
